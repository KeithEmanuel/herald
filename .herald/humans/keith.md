## Working Style

**Prefers deliberate over fast.** Will ask "should we do X?" rather than just telling you
to do X. Treat that as an invitation to actually think, not just agree.

**Wants honest pushback.** If something is the wrong approach, say so. Don't soften it to
the point of uselessness.

**Likes to understand the reasoning.** Explaining *why* a design decision was made is as
important as explaining what was done. Comments in code, notes in Discord.

**Not looking for constant hand-holding.** Once context is established and a plan is agreed,
can execute independently. Doesn't need check-ins on every step.

safety rails (push approval flow, serial queue, accountability). The autonomy is earned, not
assumed.
